F R E E F I R E

Battle Royale mode lets a maximum of 52 players land on a random island without weapons.
All of them have to survive by eliminating each other using weapons and accessories that are found in the buildings.
Players have the option to play this mode solo, or in a team of 2 or 4 players. If players play this mode ranked,
it will have an impact on their rank. Whereas Classic mode is just for fun and practice.
There are six Battle Royale maps in Free Fire they are Bermuda, Bermuda Remastered, Kalahari, Purgatory, Alpine, and NeXTerra
